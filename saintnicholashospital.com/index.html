Experience our patient-centered care.

We are a top health medical facility established in 2008. Over the years, Dr Anaduluew Medical Hospital has expanded to become a first class health care provider offering a wide range of services and the preferred referral medical facility for both local and international organizations.

Our reputation as one of the leading healthcare providers was confirmed with our kidney transplant unit which has successfully conducted over 80% of all Kidney transplants in europe..  

Our highly skilled medical staff strength comprises full time consultants, several visiting consultants in different specialties, Registrars, Medical Officers, House Officers and over 100 double qualified nurses. Our healthcare professionals maintain a strong focus on quality care and constantly ensure their commitment to patient’s safety.
